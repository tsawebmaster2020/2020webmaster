Electric Vehicles 

Electrica’s main goal is to ameliorate the increasing problem of climate change. By manufacturing electric cars for all, carbon emissions will be reduced by almost 30%. People who drive electric cars are not only protecting our planet but also saving themselves money because electric cars are low maintenance. The benefits of electric cars are limitless.








Piezoelectricity

Electrica vehicles are not only powered by traditional charging methods like other electric vehicles, but Electrica vehicles are enhanced with piezoelectric technology embedded in our custom tires. Partnered with Goodyear, Electrica’s engineers have developed one-of-a-kind piezoelectric technology that has been embedded in our tires in order to allow your car to generate its own energy as it drives. Our patented piezoelectric technology generates energy to power your vehicle through the ground contact with the tires. Whenever your vehicle is in motion, you are generating energy to fuel your ride. As a result of this technology, our vehicles last much longer than other electric cars as they do not run solely on manual charging.
