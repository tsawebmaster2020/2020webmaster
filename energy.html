Electric Vehicles 

Electrica’s main goal is to ameliorate the increasing problem of climate change. By manufacturing electric cars for all, carbon emissions will be reduced by almost 30%. People who drive electric cars are not only protecting our planet but also saving themselves money because electric cars are low maintenance. The benefits of electric cars are limitless.














Piezoelectricity

Electrica vehicles are not only powered by traditional charging methods like other electric vehicles, but Electrica vehicles are enhanced with piezoelectric technology embedded in our custom tires. Partnered with Goodyear, Electrica’s engineers have developed one-of-a-kind piezoelectric technology that has been embedded in our tires in order to allow your car to generate its own energy as it drives. Our patented piezoelectric technology generates energy to power your vehicle through the ground contact with the tires. Whenever your vehicle is in motion, you are generating energy to fuel your ride. As a result of this technology, our vehicles last much longer than other electric cars as they do not run solely on manual charging.















Reducing Carbon Emissions

Everytime you purchase an electric car, from Electrica, we become one step closer to our goal to have a world run on electricity. A gasoline-free America would reduce carbon emissions by 8.4%. A gasoline free-world would reduce carbon emissions by an even greater percent. Although 8.4% may not seem like a lot, a small percent can do a lot. An 8.4% drop in emissions gets us closer to what is needed for a carbon neutral world.











Our Future Goals

Carbon neutrality is only the beginning and we need more than just carbon neutrality if we want to ensure future generations a safe earth. If we don’t do anything to help our planet, by the end of the century, every person in the world will be facing major climate issues, such as forest fires and tsunamis. Our goal is to have every American driving electric by the year 2040. Today, our goal is to have 5 households per day to buy an electric car.

Next year, we plan to launch an 8-car line of autonomous piezoelectric cars. There will be a car for everyone, the large family, the single mother, the adventurous type, the road tripper, and the college student. An autonomous vehicle for everyone who loves cars but does not love driving.
